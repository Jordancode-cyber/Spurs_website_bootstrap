History & Background

Founded in 1882, Tottenham Hotspur Football Club is one of England's most storied football clubs. Nicknamed "Spurs," the club is based in North London and plays its home matches at the state-of-the-art Tottenham Hotspur Stadium. Spurs are known for their attacking style of play and their famous motto, "To Dare Is To Do."

The club has a rich history, including being the first British club to win a major European trophy (the Cup Winners' Cup in 1963) and achieving the league and FA Cup Double in 1961. Spurs have a passionate fanbase and a tradition of developing exciting talent.
